# Amelu

> Amelu is an email hosting service by Ordnary. It lets customers register
> or bring their own email domains and manage mailboxes, aliases, forwarding,
> spam filtering, and DNS configuration for them, on top of a self-hosted
> Stalwart mail server.

## What this application is

Amelu's dashboard is the customer-facing control panel for the service:
sign up, add an email domain, verify its DNS records, create mailboxes and
address aliases, configure spam filtering and forwarding rules, and manage
account/organization/billing settings - all without touching a mail server
directly.

## Who it's for

Individuals and organizations who want to run email on their own domain
without operating mail server infrastructure themselves.
